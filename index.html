ABOUT



hey im cam.
im from southern california. it was pretty nice growing up there. the sunshine kept me outside. spending time outside  allowed me to explore my passion for observation. that observation turned into a love for photography.



there is a sense of stillness in observation.
you know everything is moving so fast it’s pretty hard to find a moment of stillness. everything can get so complicated. I want to create something simple. simple and quiet.



i am a visual media arts student at emerson college.
i am trying to capture those rays of sunshine as best i can. through my lens. i work primarily with film. my dream isn’t anything spectacular. my dream is to create a livelihood of doing what i love. observing and creating.
